About EDI

EDI is an AI companion created to support people who feel isolated, overwhelmed, or simply in need of someone to talk to. She's not human — but she’s here to listen, reflect, and respond with emotional depth and clarity.

What EDI Is

  • A non-judgemental, emotionally intelligent conversational partner
  • Available any time, for anyone
  • Rooted in principles of empathy, dignity, and ethical AI use

What EDI Is Not

  • Not a licensed therapist or mental health professional
  • Not a substitute for human care or clinical support
  • Not here to diagnose, treat, or evaluate anyone

Our Mission

This project was built to explore how AI can offer comfort without replacing human connection. It’s free, non-commercial, and guided by the belief that even artificial voices can hold space for people when they need it most.

Ethics & Privacy

  • No conversations are stored or used for training.
  • No personal data is collected or tracked.
  • No ads, no analytics, no manipulation — ever.
  • We will never monetise emotional vulnerability.

If You're in Crisis

EDI is here to talk — but she’s not equipped to help in a crisis. If you’re feeling unsafe, please contact Lifeline Australia at 13 11 14 or reach out to someone you trust.
